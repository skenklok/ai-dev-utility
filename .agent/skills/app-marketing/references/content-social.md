## Content Marketing Strategy

### Content Funnel

```
AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)
├── Blog posts, articles
├── Social media content
├── Videos (YouTube, TikTok)
└── Infographics

CONSIDERATION (Middle of Funnel)
├── How-to guides
├── Case studies
├── Comparison content
└── Webinars, podcasts

CONVERSION (Bottom of Funnel)
├── Product tutorials
├── Testimonials
├── Feature showcases
└── Landing pages
```

### Content Types by Platform

| Content Type | Best Platforms | Effort | Impact |
|-------------|---------------|--------|--------|
| Blog/Articles | Website, Medium | Medium | SEO, authority |
| Short Videos | TikTok, Reels, Shorts | Medium | Reach, engagement |
| Long Videos | YouTube | High | SEO, depth |
| Infographics | Pinterest, LinkedIn | Medium | Shares, backlinks |
| Podcasts | Spotify, Apple | High | Authority, loyalty |
| Email Newsletter | Email | Medium | Retention, loyalty |

### SEO Content Strategy

**Keyword Research Process:**
1. Identify core topics related to your app
2. Research search volume and competition
3. Create content clusters around pillar topics
4. Target long-tail keywords for quick wins
5. Build internal linking structure

**Content Optimization Checklist:**
- [ ] Target keyword in title, URL, H1
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Related keywords naturally included
- [ ] Optimized meta description
- [ ] Alt text for images
- [ ] Internal and external links
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting

## Social Media Platforms

### Platform Selection Guide

| Platform | Best For | Audience | Content Type |
|----------|----------|----------|--------------|
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle apps | 18-44 | Photos, Stories, Reels |
| TikTok | Consumer apps, Gen Z | 16-30 | Short videos |
| Twitter/X | News, tech, B2B | 25-50 | Text, threads, links |
| LinkedIn | B2B, productivity | 25-55 | Articles, professional |
| Facebook | Broad, older demo | 30-65 | Mixed, Groups |
| YouTube | Tutorials, depth | All ages | Long-form video |
| Pinterest | Lifestyle, planning | 25-44 F | Images, guides |
| Discord | Gaming, tech, community | 16-35 | Community, chat |
| Reddit | Niche communities | 18-35 | Discussion, AMAs |

### Platform-Specific Strategies

#### Instagram
- **Feed**: High-quality visuals, carousel posts
- **Stories**: Behind-the-scenes, polls, questions
- **Reels**: Trending formats, tutorials, entertainment
- **Posting frequency**: 3-7 times/week
- **Best times**: 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm local

#### TikTok
- **Content style**: Native, authentic, entertaining
- **Trending sounds**: Use trending audio
- **Posting frequency**: 1-3 times/day for growth
- **Best times**: 7pm-9pm local, weekdays
- **Hook in first second**: Critical for retention

#### Twitter/X
- **Content style**: Threads, insights, engagement
- **Posting frequency**: 3-5 times/day
- **Engagement**: Reply to others, join conversations
- **Best times**: 8am-10am, 12pm, 5pm-6pm

#### LinkedIn
- **Content style**: Professional, educational, thought leadership
- **Posting frequency**: 1-2 times/day
- **Format**: Native posts > links, carousels work well
- **Best times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8am-10am

### Social Media Content Pillars

Create content in these categories (adapt to your app):

| Pillar | Description | % of Content |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| Educational | Tips, how-tos, tutorials | 30-40% |
| Entertaining | Memes, trends, humor | 20-30% |
| Inspiring | Success stories, quotes | 10-20% |
| Promotional | Features, launches, CTAs | 15-20% |
| Engaging | Questions, polls, UGC | 10-15% |

### Engagement Best Practices

- [ ] Respond to comments within 2-4 hours
- [ ] Ask questions to encourage engagement
- [ ] Use relevant hashtags (not too many)
- [ ] Tag relevant accounts when appropriate
- [ ] Cross-promote across platforms
- [ ] User-generated content campaigns
- [ ] Consistent posting schedule

## Influencer Marketing

### Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Follower Count | Engagement | Cost | Best For |
|------|----------------|------------|------|----------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | Very High | $100-500 | Niche, authenticity |
| Micro | 10K-100K | High | $500-2K | Targeted reach |
| Mid | 100K-500K | Medium | $2K-10K | Scaling |
| Macro | 500K-1M | Medium | $10K-50K | Broad reach |
| Mega | 1M+ | Lower | $50K+ | Mass awareness |

### Finding Influencers

**Methods:**
1. Search relevant hashtags on target platform
2. Look at competitor collaborations
3. Use influencer discovery platforms
4. Check your own followers/customers
5. Ask for referrals from current partners

**Discovery Platforms:**
- Aspire (formerly AspireIQ)
- Upfluence
- Grin
- CreatorIQ
- Heepsy
- HypeAuditor

### Influencer Evaluation Checklist

- [ ] Audience demographics match your target
- [ ] Authentic engagement (not bots)
- [ ] Content quality and style fit
- [ ] Previous brand partnerships (check quality)
- [ ] Engagement rate (likes + comments / followers)
- [ ] Audience authenticity (use tools to check)
- [ ] Platform alignment

**Engagement Rate Benchmarks:**
| Platform | Good | Excellent |
|----------|------|-----------|
| Instagram | 2-4% | 4%+ |
| TikTok | 4-6% | 6%+ |
| YouTube | 2-4% | 4%+ |
| Twitter | 0.5-1% | 1%+ |

### Collaboration Types

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Sponsored Post | One-off paid content | Awareness |
| Affiliate | Commission-based | Performance |
| Product Review | Honest review of app | Credibility |

### Measuring Influencer ROI

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| CPI | Spend / Installs | Below avg. paid CPI |
| CPM | (Spend / Impressions) × 1000 | $5-20 |
| Engagement Rate | (Likes + Comments) / Reach | Above platform avg. |
| Conversion Rate | Clicks / Installs | 20%+ |

## Community Building

### Community Platforms

| Platform | Best For | Effort | Control |
|----------|----------|--------|---------|
| Discord | Gaming, tech, engaged communities | High | Full |
| Facebook Groups | Broad audiences, older demo | Medium | Medium |
| Slack | B2B, professional | Medium | Full |
| Circle | Paid communities, courses | Medium | Full |
| Reddit (Subreddit) | Niche topics | High | Limited |
| In-app Community | Deep engagement | Highest | Full |

### Community Strategy Framework

**Phase 1: Foundation (0-100 members)**
- Define community purpose and values
- Create welcome/onboarding experience
- Seed with engaging content
- Personal outreach to early members

**Phase 2: Growth (100-1,000 members)**
- Establish rituals (weekly events, challenges)
- Identify and empower super members
- Create sub-groups or channels
- Launch referral programs

**Phase 3: Scale (1,000+ members)**
- Community-led content and events
- Moderation team/volunteers
- User-generated content programs
- Feedback loops to product

### Community Engagement Ideas

- [ ] Weekly AMAs or Q&As
- [ ] User challenges with prizes
- [ ] Feature sneak peeks
- [ ] Bug hunting/beta testing
- [ ] User spotlight/stories
- [ ] Poll-based feature decisions
- [ ] Community content creation

## PR & Press

### Press Strategy

**When to do PR:**
- App launch
- Major feature releases
- Funding announcements
- Milestone achievements
- Industry awards
- Thought leadership

### Press Kit Components

```
PRESS KIT
├── Press release (1-2 pages)
├── Fact sheet (key stats, info)
├── Founder bios and headshots
├── App screenshots (high-res)
├── App icon (various sizes)
├── Logo files (various formats)
├── Demo video or GIF
└── Press mentions/testimonials
```

### Finding Press Contacts

**Methods:**
1. Research journalists who cover your category
2. Use tools like Muck Rack, HARO, Cision
3. Follow and engage on Twitter
4. Check bylines on relevant articles
5. Look at who covered competitors

### Pitch Email Template

```
Subject: [Short, compelling hook about your app]

Hi [Name],

[1 sentence about why you're reaching out to them specifically]

[2-3 sentences about your app and what makes it newsworthy]

Key highlights:
- [Impressive stat or achievement]
- [Unique feature or angle]
- [Relevance to their audience]

I'd love to [offer: demo, interview, exclusive, etc.].

[Link to press kit]

Best,
[Your name]
```

### PR Best Practices

**Do:**
- ✅ Personalize each pitch
- ✅ Keep emails short (<150 words)
- ✅ Lead with the news angle
- ✅ Follow up once (after 3-5 days)
- ✅ Build relationships before you need coverage

**Don't:**
- ❌ Mass blast press releases
- ❌ Use jargon or hyperbole
- ❌ Attach large files (link instead)
- ❌ Follow up more than twice
- ❌ Ignore journalists' beats

### Tracking Coverage

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Coverage volume | Awareness reach |
| Domain authority | SEO impact |
| Referral traffic | Direct impact |
| Social shares | Amplification |
| Backlinks | SEO value |
| Sentiment | Brand perception |
