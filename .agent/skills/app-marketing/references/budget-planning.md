# Marketing Budget Planning Guide

Framework for app marketing budget allocation.

## Budget Fundamentals

### How Much to Spend

| Stage | Marketing % of Budget |
|-------|----------------------|
| Pre-revenue | 100% of marketing budget |
| Launch (0-6 months) | 30-50% of total budget |
| Growth (6-18 months) | 20-40% of revenue |
| Mature | 10-20% of revenue |

> Successful apps allocate 20-40% of budget to marketing.

### Budget Components

| Category | % of Budget | Purpose |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Paid Acquisition | 40-60% | User growth |
| Creative & Production | 10-15% | Ad assets |
| Organic Marketing | 10-20% | Content, SEO |
| PR & Partnerships | 5-15% | Awareness |
| Tools & Analytics | 5-10% | Measurement |
| Testing Reserve | 5-10% | Experiments |

---

## Allocation by Stage

### Pre-Launch ($5K-25K)
- Landing Page & Tools: 10-15%
- Content Creation: 20-30%
- Social Media: 15-20%
- PR & Influencer: 20-30%
- Testing Reserve: 10-15%

### Launch Phase ($10K-50K/month)
- Paid Acquisition: 40-50%
- ASO & Creative: 15-20%
- PR & Influencers: 15-20%
- Content/Social: 10-15%
- Tools: 5-10%

### Growth Phase ($25K-100K+/month)
- Paid Acquisition: 50-60%
- Retention Marketing: 10-15%
- Creative Production: 10-15%
- Testing: 5-10%

---

## LTV:CAC Targets

| Ratio | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| <1:1 | Losing money | Stop/reduce |
| 1:1-2:1 | Break-even | Optimize |
| 2:1-3:1 | Healthy | Maintain |
| 3:1+ | Efficient | Scale |

---

## Scaling Rules

1. Increase budget 20-30%/week max
2. Monitor for efficiency decay
3. Scale horizontally before vertically
4. Refresh creative regularly
5. Expand audiences gradually

---

## Monthly Review Process

1. Rank channels by ROAS/LTV
2. Cut bottom 20% of spend
3. Reallocate to top performers
4. Reserve 10% for tests
